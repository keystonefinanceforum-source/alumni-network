The Keystone Finance Forum is the premier student-run finance institution developing the next generation of financial thinkers. We build the person who analyse, question, and engage with finance at a level that goes far beyond the classroom — and a professional network of driven minds built to last well beyond academics.
Our Three Pillars
01

Financial Intelligence

Building strong financial thinking through market awareness, investing basics, business analysis, and real-world economic understanding that goes beyond textbooks.

02

Leadership & Initiative

Creating future leaders by encouraging public speaking, teamwork, event management, networking, and decision-making through student-led projects and discussions.

03

Innovation & Impact

Connecting finance with innovation, entrepreneurship, and social impact by exploring emerging industries, startup culture, and ideas that shape the future economy.
